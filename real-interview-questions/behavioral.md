# 🧠 Behavioral & Leadership — Real Interview Questions

> Curated from real interview experiences shared on LinkedIn.
> These are commonly asked alongside technical rounds.

---

## 🔹 Ownership & Decision Making

1. **Have you led a team?**

2. **Do you break down requirements?**

3. **Have you made architectural decisions?**
   - _Also asked as:_ "You said architected — draw the architecture on paper right now."

4. **What's the hardest technical decision you made alone?**

---

## 🔹 Conflict & Communication

5. **Tell me about a time you disagreed with your team.**

6. **How do you resolve implementation conflicts with design or backend teams?**

7. **How would you handle a team and distribute work based on skills?**

8. **What factors would you consider while selecting team members?**

9. **How do you mentor junior developers and review code?**

10. **Tell me about a time you disagreed with product or design.**
   - _Also asked as:_ How do you handle pushback from product/design teams?

---

## 🔹 Production Incidents

9. **Tell me about a production bug you caused.**

10. **Share a production UI failure incident — what was your mitigation and rollback plan?**

11. **Walk me through the hardest bug you fixed in production.**

---

## 🔹 Delivery & Shipping

12. **Describe a feature you shipped under strict deadlines — how did you scope and deliver?**

13. **Tell me about a complex UI you built.**

14. **What would you do differently if you rebuilt your project today?**

15. **When did you rescue a failing feature?**
    - _Also asked as:_ Tell me about a time you took ownership of something broken.

16. **A real performance bug you fixed in production — root cause and approach.**

17. **What would you change about [company's app] right now?**
    - _Also asked as:_ Use the app for 15 minutes before the manager round and have one specific improvement ready.

---

## 🔹 Career & Self

15. **Tell me about yourself and your experience in frontend development.**

16. **Why did you leave your previous company?**

17. **Where do you see yourself in 2 years?**

18. **Explain your current project architecture.**
    - _Also asked as:_ Describe your role and responsibilities.

19. **What challenges did you face during development and how did you solve them?**

---

## 🔹 Trap Questions

20. **"You used a lot of buzzwords — explain one from scratch, no jargon."**

21. **"Your AI integration is just API calls — why should I be impressed?"**
